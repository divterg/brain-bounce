ⓘ How to Use

  1. Put on stereo headphones. This is essential for the left and right movement.
  2. Choose a built-in sound, upload audio, or use microphone input.
  3. Adjust bounce speed. Slower settings feel calmer. Faster settings feel more active.
  4. Adjust intensity. This controls how far the sound travels between ears.
  5. Close your eyes and let the bilateral movement guide the listening session.
Important: This app is for relaxation, focus support, and audio experimentation. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.
Keep Awake: When you press play, the app will try to keep your iPhone screen awake during the session. If iOS blocks it, set Auto-Lock to Never while using the app.
The science ⌃

Why it works

🧠

Bilateral Stimulation

Alternating audio engages left and right attention channels, similar in concept to bilateral techniques used in guided therapies.

⌖

Focus Support

Rhythmic movement can give the mind a steady anchor, which may help some listeners settle racing thoughts.

ϟ

Adaptive Listening

Repeated sessions may help users build familiar focus rituals around sound, breath, and attention.

✧

Deep Relaxation

The 8D panning effect creates an immersive experience that may support calm and presence.

Best experienced with stereo headphones. Keep volume safe. Stop using it if you feel dizzy, uncomfortable, or overstimulated.
