ⓘ How to Use

  1. Put on stereo headphones. The bounce depends on left and right separation.
  2. Tap the power button first. iPhone Safari requires a tap before web audio can start.
  3. Choose a built-in sound or upload an audio file.
  4. Set Bounce Depth to 100% for the strongest left-right movement.
  5. Set Bounce Speed slower for calm sessions or faster for active focus.
Keep Awake: The app requests screen wake lock during playback. If iOS still dims or locks, use Settings > Display & Brightness > Auto-Lock > Never during sessions.
Important: This app is for relaxation, focus support, and audio experimentation. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.
